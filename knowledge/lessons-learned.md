## Adding New Lessons

**Template:**

```markdown
## LESS-W###: [Lesson Title] [Status Tag]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Source:** [Retrospective/Incident/Version]
**Context:** [What were you doing when you learned this]

**What We Learned:**
[The key insight - 1-2 sentences]

**Evidence:**
- [Data point 1]
- [Data point 2]
- [Observations]

**Why This Matters:**
[Impact of ignoring this lesson]

**Action Taken:**
[What we changed as a result]

**Impact:**
[Results of the change]

**Related:**
- [Link to related docs]

**Tags:** [Version-specific/Pending Review/Adopted]
```

**When to Create:**
- After retrospectives
- After learning from mistakes
- When pattern emerges across versions
- When discovering better approach

**Promotion Criteria:**
- **[Version-specific]:** Applies to one version only
- **[Pending Review]:** Might apply to other versions, needs validation
- **[Adopted]:** Validated across 2+ versions or workspace-wide impact
